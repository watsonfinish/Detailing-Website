Exterior Refresh Starting at $85

Exterior Refresh

A strong maintenance package for vehicles that need a cleaner look, fresh shine, and routine exterior upkeep without going into a full deep detail.

  • Hand wash of the full exterior
  • Wheel and tire cleaning
  • Tire shine application
  • Exterior glass cleaning
  • Quick spray protection for gloss and surface enhancement
  • Final wipe-down for a cleaner presentation

Why this is a good plan for your car: It helps maintain a clean, presentable exterior, supports paint appearance between deeper services, and is a solid recurring option for regular upkeep.

Interior Reset Starting at $110

Interior Reset

A deeper interior-focused package for vehicles that need dirt, crumbs, dust, and light buildup cleaned out to make the cabin feel fresher and more comfortable.

  • Full interior vacuum
  • Wipe down of major interior surfaces
  • Dash, console, and door panel cleaning
  • Interior glass cleaning
  • Floor mat refresh
  • Finishing touch to improve overall cabin appearance

Why this is a good plan for your car: It improves day-to-day comfort, keeps the inside looking more cared for, and is great when the cabin needs more than a quick cleanup but not a full premium detail.

Full Detail Starting at $185

Full Detail

This is the best overall package for customers who want the strongest visual improvement and the most complete inside-and-out refresh in one appointment.

  • Everything included in Exterior Refresh
  • Everything included in Interior Reset
  • Clay bar treatment as needed
  • Sealant or wax protection for extra gloss
  • More complete finishing touch-up
  • Stronger overall transformation in appearance

Why this is a good plan for your car: It gives the best value, provides the most noticeable improvement, and works well for vehicles that need a more complete reset rather than light maintenance only.
